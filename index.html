UX Process I Follow

Below are some of the "User Centered Design" process i do follow to conceptualize the Mental Model

  • To finding usability problems in a user interface

  • UCD focuses on conceptualizing the mental model

  • To understand the business requirement and user group

  • To Identify business intent, user, critical success factor & technical limitations

  • They are the real users who would be part of design journey

  • Interact with real users to understand their needs & goals

  • To design the users navigation patterns and the labels

  • Documenting the users mental model in a story format

  • Breakdown the fewer decision points from scenarios

  • Skeleton of a page that reflects navigation & content presentation

  • It's an working model that helps to show the ideas & intentions behind the design concept to the clients & users

  • Done at every stage of design to identify the usability issues
